JSW Neosteel

JSW Neosteel is a superior quality TMT bar with higher strength coupled with flexibility. What sets JSW Neosteel apart is the manufacturing process that ensures a product of unmatched quality. To begin with, it is made from virgin iron ore, which gives it the highest grade of purity. Then, there’s the state-of-the-art rolling process, which imparts uniform properties across the bar.

Even though these bars have high strength due to their inherent microstructure that comes with a soft ferrite and pearlite core, these TMT bars are bendable into customised shapes. JSW Neosteel, because of its unique rib pattern, bonds firmly with concrete, creating a life-long bond. It is made through state-of-the-art MORGAN technology, which is known for producing high-quality HYQST (High Yield Quenched and Self Tempered) bars through METCS (Morgan Enhanced Temperature Control System) process.

Introduced in 2009 as JSW TMT Plus, it was later rebranded as JSW Neosteel in 2015, with a revised brand proposition of Pure TMT Bars, evolving from the earlier positioning of Premium TMT Bar.

In 2020, we launched a new variant – JSW Neosteel 550D – with enhanced strength and flexibility. It was introduced with the campaign “Better Strength, More Savings” to highlight cost savings from reduced steel consumption.
